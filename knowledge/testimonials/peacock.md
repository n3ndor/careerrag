Dr. Christopher Peacock, Chief Information Officer and Chief Architect at 3BX
(Books Beats Box, Inc.), wrote a formal recommendation letter for Nandor in
October 2023. Nandor describes him as "my first job offer and a trusted
mentor". The letter, in full:

"Nandor Nagy worked for me as a Frontend software developer as a member of the
3BX development team (BooksBeatsBox). During 2023 he was very responsive and
engaged in developing code for the 3BX products and web site. I was impressed
by his ability to engage with other members of the team to determine what was
needed. He would gather, analyze, and design complex Web applications to
achieve the development results required.

Using a variety of technologies, without hesitation he would move forward to
develop the framework and then the product itself. During this time, Nandor
proved himself to be a dependable employee and a hard worker with solid
problem-solving and technical skills. I was always impressed by his ability to
complete the work assignments. Nandor not only met deadlines but also took on
additional tasks that were needed.

Nandor takes his work very seriously and was a fast-learner, careful yet
cautious about his work. He repeatedly ran checks on his programs (and others
he ran across) before saving them in the development site for upload to
production. His quick thinking and cautious nature caught many potentially
costly issues needing correction. Nandor would be an asset to have on any
team. He went out of his way to ensure things worked well together. I would
recommend Nandor and welcome him on any team I was associated with. Not only
for his professional skills, efficiency, speed, and smooth functionality but
for his engaging personality."
