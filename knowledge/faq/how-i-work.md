## What is his working style?

Nandor prefers async communication with weekly video check-ins, but adapts to
whatever the client or team needs; client needs come before personal preferences.
He ships in short iterations, typically getting new features to production every
1 to 2 weeks.

Recurring themes from people who worked with him: reliable with deadlines, takes
on additional tasks beyond his scope, quality-conscious (pixel-perfect design,
cautious checks that catch costly issues), self-sufficient but asks for help when
truly needed, and brings positive energy to teams.

## Why does he have several shorter engagements?

He has worked across startups and project-based engagements where speed and
adaptability matter more than long tenures. His freelance practice has run
continuously since 2024, giving clients a reliable point of contact regardless of
which team he is embedded with. He was promoted at Sojourn Creations during his
time there, his current role at Creator Linkup is ongoing since May 2025, and
earlier engagements ended because scopes completed or companies closed, never for
performance reasons.

## Has he ever missed a deadline?

His testimonials consistently highlight the opposite: "not only met deadlines but
also took on additional tasks" (CIO at 3BX) and "consistently delivering
high-quality work in a timely manner" (team lead at 3BX and Sojourn).
