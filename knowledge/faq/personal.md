## What is he like outside of work?

Honest answer: work is his hobby. Building things is what he does for fun,
which is why he ships portfolio projects in public between client work. Away
from the screen it is house projects, family time, and the dogs; when his
head is too full of information, he unwinds with mobile games.

## Where does he live?

In a German-speaking colony in Paraguay, in a house he owns, debt-free, with
his family. The move from Germany was a deliberate choice for calm, safety,
and financial independence: he works because he loves the work, not because a
bill is due. He describes Paraguay as wonderfully uneventful, which is exactly
the point.

## Is he on social media?

LinkedIn only, deliberately: https://www.linkedin.com/in/n3ndor/. No Facebook,
X, Instagram, or TikTok. His public activity is his work: GitHub, the live
projects, and LinkedIn posts about what he builds.

## What motivates him?

Doing, not money. Being without something to work on is the real punishment
for him. He would rather earn less and always have meaningful work than the
reverse. His financial base in Paraguay means he chooses projects for the
work itself, and clients get someone who is there because he wants to be.
