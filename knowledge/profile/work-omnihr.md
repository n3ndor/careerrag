Nandor worked at OmniHR, a US company (remote), from January 2024 to August 2024
as a Full Stack Developer. The company wanted an HR platform without building one
from scratch, so he was brought in to evaluate existing open-source solutions,
pick the best fit, and customize it.

What he did there:

- Evaluated and deployed 10+ open-source HR platforms (including MintHR and
  OrangeHR) on AWS EC2 so the team could test each one thoroughly.
- Customized the selected platform with React and Node.js: personalized branding,
  content, and updated functions to match company needs.
- Mentored 5-6 interns with little prior coding experience on JavaScript and Git.
- Produced documentation to guide the final technology decision.

The engagement ended when the company closed; it was a completed scope, not
performance related.
