Nandor worked at BooksBeatsBox (3BX), a US company (remote), from July 2023 to
March 2024 as a Front-End Developer. 3BX is a social media platform and digital
marketplace for independent creators: authors, musicians, artists, and
influencers. The mission was to give indie creators their first step before
platforms like Amazon. Around 40 people worked on it, with teams across the US,
India, and China.

What he built there:

- Personally created approximately 60 pages (including modals, steppers, and UI
  components) across the web platform.
- Polished the UI/UX across the entire site.
- Built a JavaScript music player prototype backed by RESTful APIs.
- Worked with international teams across India and China.
- Managed 120+ Trello tasks in agile sprints.

His leads there were Dr. Christopher Peacock (CIO/Chief Architect) and Brendan
Cordova (Lead Full Stack Developer). Both wrote strong recommendation letters,
and Brendan later chose to work with Nandor again at Sojourn Creations.
