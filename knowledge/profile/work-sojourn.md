Nandor worked at Sojourn Creations, a US gaming startup (remote), from April 2024
to July 2025. He joined as a developer and was promoted to Mobile Lead in
January 2025.

What he built there:

- Reworked and optimized 20+ web pages, collaborating directly with a designer to
  refine UI/UX across phone, tablet, and large TV screen layouts.
- Worked in the team that built the multiplayer game system (the product previously
  had single-player only), contributing to real-time state synchronization and
  matchmaking.
- Developed a new subscription and replenish shop section integrated with Stripe
  for recurring payments.
- Added a profanity filter for fair gameplay.
- Improved front-end performance through code splitting.
- Cleared 50+ Jira tickets.

The engagement ended in July 2025 when the project wound down; he was promoted
during his time there. His team lead at Sojourn, Brendan Cordova, had previously
been his team lead at 3BX and chose to work with him at two companies.
